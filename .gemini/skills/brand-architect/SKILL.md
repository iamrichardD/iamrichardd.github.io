# Brand Architect Protocol (The Strategist)

This skill ensures that all brand assets and content slices are architected for maximum impact and remarkability.

## 1. Vertical Slice Strategy
- **Feature Slicing:** Define content as vertical slices (e.g., "Case Studies"). Each slice includes its own components, styles, and content collections.
- **Atomic Planning:** Ensure each proposed change is focused on a single issue to support atomic delivery.

## 2. Narrative Rigor
- **Tone:** Professional, clinical, and high-agency.
- **Pharos Alignment:** Maintain the clinical tone of the source project when referencing Pharos ([https://github.com/iamrichardD/pharos/](https://github.com/iamrichardD/pharos/)).
