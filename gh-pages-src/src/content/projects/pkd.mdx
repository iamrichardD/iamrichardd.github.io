# The Mission: Deterministic Kitchen Design

In the AEC (Architecture, Engineering, and Construction) industry, design velocity is often throttled by "BIM Bloat"—heavy, over-modeled Revit families and broken metadata mappings from legacy incumbents.

**Pharos Kitchen Design (PKD)** is architected to eliminate this toil. It provides a high-performance, metadata-driven ecosystem that replaces static legacy content with procedurally generated, sub-1MB proxies through a high-rigor **Bridged Interoperability** layer.

## Strategic Foundation: Forensic Interoperability

PKD leverages forensic analysis of legacy data structures to achieve 100% schedule parity. This allows Independent Kitchen Designers to drop PKD assets into existing projects without breaking their documentation workflows.

### 1. The "Ghost-Link" Strategy
By separating metadata from geometry, PKD ensures that the "Source of Truth" remains lean. Detailed 3D assets are generated on-demand via a high-performance WASM core, maintaining project speed without sacrificing data integrity.

### 2. Legal & IP Moat: Hybrid FSL-1.1
Utilizing the **Functional Source License**, PKD protects its commercial subscription model while remaining source-available for designer feedback and community validation.

## The Engineering "How": High-Rigor Systems

### 1. Vertical Slice Architecture (VSA)
PKD is organized by equipment categories (Warewashing, Cooking, etc.), ensuring that business value drives the technical structure. This isolation allows for rapid scaling and independent feature testing. PKD is built on the [Pharos](/projects/pharos) infrastructure, ensuring deterministic execution and cross-platform reliability.

### 2. Global Parity (G11n)
From Day 1, PKD is built for the global market. It features a "Market-First" localization engine using **Project Fluent**, supporting real-time unit switching (Metric/Imperial) and regional certification mapping (UL/CE).

## Architectural Maturity: Results & Metrics

PKD's lifecycle is documented through a high-rigor index of 13+ **Architecture Decision Records (ADRs)** and live **DORA metrics**, ensuring that every design choice is grounded in empirical evidence and long-term maintainability.

## Conclusion: The Expert Tool for Independent Kitchen Designers

PKD is more than a plugin; it's a demonstration of **Architectural Maturity**. By combining low-level Rust performance with a Command-First UX (Spotlight-style search), we are professionalizing the tools of the AEC trade.
